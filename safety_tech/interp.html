At a Glance

Subject: Mechanistic interpretability of large language models and deep neural networks.

Scope: Feature attribution, circuit discovery, probing, sparse autoencoders, causal tracing.

Audience: ML researchers, AI safety practitioners, technical readers.

Key Definitions
  • Feature
    A direction in activation space representing a concept.
  • Circuit
    A subgraph of model components implementing a specific algorithm.
  • Superposition
    Storage of n features in d < n dims via near-orthogonal coding.
  • Faithfulness
    Degree to which an explanation reflects actual model computation.
