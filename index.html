About This Book

Building Safe intelligence is an analytical monograph examining the structural risks, governance challenges, and institutional responses associated with advanced AI systems.

Grounded in a realistic horizon of 3 life times, it is intended for informed generalist readers, policy practitioners, and researchers approaching AI risks.

Chapters are released as drafts and revised iteratively. Feedback is incorporated into published revisions.

Core Themes
Latent Risk Harms that accrue through structural drift rather than acute events.
Optimization Dynamics How capable optimizers behave under consistent objectives at scale.
Institutional Lag The gap between AI capability trajectories and governance response times.
The Agency Question What changes when AI systems act in the world, not just advise on it.
Analytical Scope

This series is non restrictive and expanded with subsequent iterations
